The Problem

Companies running AI models in production on AWS, GCP, or Azure are paying 2–4× too much per GPU-hour. The hyperscalers price inference like a scarce enterprise product — it isn't anymore. But the cheaper capacity that exists is fragmented, unmanaged, and a headache to operate.

The Solution

Heliode sources wholesale GPU capacity and runs it end to end:

  • One managed endpoint — OpenAI-compatible, their same API.
  • One bill, real support — no infra to babysit.
  • A fraction of on-demand cloud rates on the same H100/H200 hardware.

We earn margin over wholesale spot, with committed-spend tiers as customers scale.

Why Now

  • Inference demand is compounding; every team needs steady GPU-hours.
  • Capacity is splintering across neoclouds & marketplaces — buyers can't navigate it.
  • Teams want hyperscaler simplicity at open-market prices. Nobody owns that lane.

Model & Roadmap

  • Next: a prototype owned node in Austin to prove unit economics.
